# Analysis

## Layer 2, Head 1

This head follows a subject across two verbs joined by and (turned, passed); (went, ordered). The subject pays the most attention to the second verb in the sentence, effectively tracking both things the person is doing, not just the most accessible/nearest one. This makes it stand out from a pattern in which it would just look at the next word, since it skips over any conjunctions or objects, etc. to find the second verb. It's efficient! :3

Example Sentences:
- We turned down a narrow lane and passed through a small field.
- I went to the new diner and ordered a burger.
